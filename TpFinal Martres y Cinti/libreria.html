FORTNITE MY LIBRARY FAQ

WHAT IS FORTNITES "MY LIBRARY"?

Fortnites “My Library” is where you can quickly see your favorite and recently played islands. If you’re looking at the in-game version of My Library, you can select one of your favorite or recently played islands and start playing!

CAN I SAVE MY FAVORITE FORTNITE ISLANDS TO MY LIBRARY?

Yes, the islands you favorite will appear in your library. Click the “heart” icon for an island (either on the islands webpage or in-game) to favorite it.

DO I NEED TO BE LOGGED INTO FORTNITES.COM TO SEE MY LIBRARY ON WEB?

To access the web version of My Library, you need to be logged into Fortnite.com. Log into Fortnite.com with the same Epic account you use to play Fortnite.

IS THERE A DIFFERENCE BETWEEN THW WEB AND IN-GAME VERSIONS OF FORTNITES MY LIBRARY?

The web version of My Library displays the islands you’ve added to your playlist. In-game, your playlist appears in “Discover” instead. (In the “My Playlist” row.)
